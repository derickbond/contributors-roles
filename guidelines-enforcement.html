Guidelines

Confidentiality

The project should not be shared with anyone outside the project team without prior authorization. All project-related discussions, documents, and information should be kept confidential and within the project team.

Active Participation

All project members are expected to actively contribute to the project's development and progress. Regular engagement in discussions, meetings, and assigned tasks is required.

Timely Delivery

Project members should complete their assigned tasks within agreed-upon timelines. Timely delivery of work ensures smooth progress and overall project success.

Communication

Maintain open and transparent communication within the project team. Utilize project management tools, instant messaging platforms, and regular meetings to facilitate effective communication channels.

Accountability

Each member is accountable for their assigned tasks and responsibilities. Take ownership of the work and ensure high-quality outputs.

Respect and Professionalism

Treat all team members with respect, professionalism, and courtesy. Maintain a positive and inclusive work environment that encourages collaboration and constructive feedback.

Compliance with Guidelines

Adhere to project guidelines, coding standards, and best practices. Follow established project processes and workflows to maintain consistency and efficiency.

Enforcement Measures

Performance Assessment

Regularly assess the performance and contribution of project members. Identify areas of non-compliance or lack of contribution.

Feedback and Support

Provide feedback and constructive criticism to members who are not meeting their obligations. Offer support, guidance, or additional resources to help improve performance.

Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs)

If non-compliance or lack of contribution persists, implement a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP). A PIP outlines specific expectations, timelines, and consequences if improvement is not demonstrated.

Removal of Non-Contributing Members

In cases of consistent non-compliance or lack of contribution, non-contributing members may be removed from the project team. Removal will be done after providing sufficient opportunities and support for improvement.
