Role Descriptions

UI/UX Designer

Responsible for creating visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces for the platform. Conducts user research and usability testing to gather feedback and make improvements. Collaborates with the development team to ensure seamless integration of design elements.

Front-End Developer

Implements the user interface design using web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Collaborates with designers to ensure accurate translation of design mockups into functional web pages. Ensures the responsiveness and performance optimization of the front-end components.

Back-End Developer

Develops and maintains the server-side logic of the platform using languages like Python, PHP, or Java. Implements database structures and ensures efficient data handling. Collaborates with the front-end developers to integrate the user interface with the server-side functionality.

Full-Stack Developer

Works on both the front-end and back-end development of the platform. Responsible for implementing features, optimizing performance, and ensuring smooth functionality across the entire system.

Graphic Designer

Creates visually compelling graphics and illustrations for various platform components. Designs marketing materials, banners, and branding elements to promote the platform. Collaborates with the UI/UX designer to ensure visual consistency and a cohesive user experience.

Content Writer

Generates engaging and informative content for the platform, including product descriptions, blog posts, and marketing copy. Conducts research on industry trends and writes compelling content to attract and engage users. Collaborates with the marketing team to develop content strategies and enhance the platform's visibility.

Project Coordinator

Oversees the coordination and scheduling of project tasks. Tracks project progress, identifies potential issues, and ensures timely completion of deliverables. Facilitates communication and collaboration among team members, stakeholders, and external resources.

Financial Coordinator

Manages project finances, including budgeting, expense tracking, and financial reporting. Works closely with the project manager and stakeholders to ensure proper allocation of resources. Monitors financial performance, identifies cost-saving opportunities, and ensures compliance with financial guidelines.

Administrative Coordinator

Provides administrative support to the project team, including scheduling meetings, managing documentation, and organizing project-related activities. Assists in coordinating communication and logistics between team members, stakeholders, and external partners. Helps maintain project documentation, including meeting minutes, progress reports, and action items.

Project Manager

Leads and oversees the overall planning, execution, and delivery of the project. Manages project scope, schedule, and budget. Coordinates with team members, stakeholders, and external partners to ensure project success. Provides guidance and support to team members and facilitates effective communication throughout the project lifecycle.
